Our Commitment to Sustainability

Eco-Friendly Materials

We use 100% natural soy wax sourced from sustainable farms. Unlike paraffin wax, soy wax is renewable, biodegradable, and burns cleaner for your home.

Recyclable Packaging

All our candles come in recyclable glass jars that can be reused for storage, decorative purposes, or recycled responsibly. We minimize plastic use and use eco-friendly materials throughout our packaging.

Local Sourcing

We partner with local suppliers and artisans, reducing our carbon footprint while supporting the Indian economy. By choosing Candles, you support local communities.

Our Environmental Goals

  • ♻️ 100% recyclable packaging by 2026
  • 🌿 Carbon-neutral production by 2027
  • 🌍 Plant one tree for every order
  • 💚 Support sustainable farming communities
