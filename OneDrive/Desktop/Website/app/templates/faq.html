Frequently Asked Questions

How long do your candles burn?

Our candles typically burn for 40-55 hours depending on the size and type. To maximize burn time, trim the wick to ¼ inch before each use and avoid drafts.

What materials do you use?

We use 100% pure soy wax, natural essential oils, and eco-friendly packaging. All materials are sourced responsibly and are cruelty-free.

How does the scent last?

Our therapeutic blends are formulated to release gradually throughout the candle's life. For best results, allow the top layer to melt completely on first burn to prevent tunneling.

What's your return policy?

We offer a 30-day satisfaction guarantee. If you're not happy with your purchase, contact us for a full refund or replacement.

Simply select COD at checkout. Our delivery partner will contact you to confirm. You can pay with cash when the package arrives. No hidden charges!

We ship within 2-3 business days. Pan-India delivery typically takes 5-7 days depending on your location.

Absolutely! We use renewable soy wax, recyclable glass jars, and eco-friendly packaging. We also plant one tree for every order placed.
