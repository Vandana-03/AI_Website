About Candle Bliss

Our Story

Candles was born from a simple dream: to create handcrafted candles that bring wellness, joy, and warmth to homes across India. Each candle is poured with love and intention, using only the finest sustainable materials.

Our Mission

We believe in the power of mindfulness and self-care. Our mission is to provide therapeutic candles that help you create moments of peace, clarity, and connection in your everyday life.

  • ✓ Eco-friendly and recyclable packaging
  • ✓ Therapeutic benefits backed by scent science
  • ✓ Made in India with local pride
  • ✓ 30-day satisfaction guarantee

Artisan Process

Every Candles candle goes through a careful handcrafting process:

  1. Selection of premium soy wax and essential oils
  2. Blending therapeutic scents for specific benefits
  3. Hand-pouring at precise temperatures
  4. Cooling and curing for optimal quality
  5. Quality inspection and packaging with love
